ALL GUESTS (CHILDREN & ADULTS) MUST WEAR SOCKS. WHEN IN BABY AREA OR INSIDE JUMPY HOUSE SOCKS MAY BE PURCHASED.

A $150 NON-REFUNDABLE DEPOSIT IS DUE UPON BOOKING A PARTY OR PRIVATE EVENT. IT WILL BE APPLIED TO YOUR FINAL BILL WHICH MUST BE PAID IN FULL AT OR PRIOR TO THE START OF YOUR EVENT.

EVENTS MAY BE RESCHEDULED ONE TIME AT LEAST 7 DAYS PRIOR TO THE EVENT. AFTER THE 7TH DAY, $150 DEPOSIT WILL BE FORFEITED.

FAQ

What ages can play here?

We welcome children ages 1-12!

Walk-ins are welcome, but booking is recommended for parties and busy weekends.

Are socks required?

Yes, socks are required for all guests. You can bring your own or purchase at the front desk.

Can I bring outside food?

Outside food is not allowed except for birthday cakes with a party booking.
